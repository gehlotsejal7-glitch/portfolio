Design Process

How I Design

Every great product starts with understanding people. Here's how I approach design — from blank canvas to shipped experience.

01
🔍
Understand
Deep dive into the problem space. Who are the users? What are their goals, frustrations, and mental models? I start with empathy before touching any tool.
02
📊
Research
User interviews, competitive analysis, heuristic evaluations. Data and insights shape every design decision — intuition backed by evidence.
03
✏️
Wireframe
Low-fidelity sketches and digital wireframes to explore structure without visual noise. Focus on flow, hierarchy, and information architecture.
04
⚡
Prototype
Interactive prototypes that bring ideas to life. Test assumptions early, fail fast, and iterate before expensive development begins.
05
🔁
Refine
Iterate based on user feedback. Polish typography, spacing, color, and micro-interactions. Good design is 10% inspiration, 90% iteration.
06
🚀
Deliver
Handoff with precision — design systems, component specs, developer notes. Design doesn't end at handoff; it lives in the shipped product.
